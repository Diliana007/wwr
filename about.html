Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver safe, thrilling, and unforgettable rafting experiences. Join us as we navigate the wild rapids of the world's most beautiful rivers!

History

Founded in 1999 by river enthusiast John Waters, Splash White Water Rafting Co. has grown from a single raft operation to one of the premier rafting companies in the region. What started as a passion project has transformed into a thriving business that has introduced over 50,000 adventurers to the thrill of white water rafting. Our experienced guides have navigated rivers across three continents and bring that wealth of knowledge to every trip.
